About

What is OwoAI?

OwoAI is a knowledge-based AI platform offering simple, accessible and multilingual financial and savings guidance to low-income and unbanked individuals ‚Äì especially young people and women. It transforms complex financial terms into everyday language, so anyone can learn how to manage money with confidence.

What OwoAI teaches

  • Money management tips

    Learn how to track spending, avoid common money mistakes and make better day-to-day financial decisions.

  • Savings goals & habit building

    Turn small daily amounts into meaningful goals like rent, school fees or business capital through consistent saving.

  • Budgeting made simple

    Break income into clear portions for needs, wants and savings using practical examples tailored to low and irregular income.

  • Nano-loans & remittances

    Understand how small loans, mobile money and remittance services work, so users can borrow and receive funds safely.

Multilingual, accessible and safe

Most financial education materials are only available in English. OwoAI is designed to close this gap by speaking the language of the community ‚Äì literally.
